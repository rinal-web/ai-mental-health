Our Visionary Developer

Passion, creativity, and dedication driving innovation

Behind this project is a passionate young innovator who believes in using technology to create a positive impact on society. Through creativity, curiosity, and dedication, this project was developed to explore how Artificial Intelligence and robotics can support human well-being.

The development of this project involved research, design, coding, and testing to build a meaningful solution that focuses on improving both mental health awareness and physical fitness. This work reflects the idea that technology should not only be advanced but also compassionate and helpful to people.

Our Core Focus

  • Emotional Intelligence – Understanding human emotions through AI.
  • Preventive Well-being – Identifying stress and mood changes early.
  • Smart Health Support – Providing guidance for mental and physical wellness.
  • Innovation for Humanity – Using technology to create meaningful social impact.

Mohammed Hasen Khan

AI & Robotics Developer

Mohammed Hasen Khan is the creator and developer of the AI Mental Health and Fitness Check Robo project. He designed and programmed the system using block-based coding in PictoBlox, integrating Artificial Intelligence to detect human emotions and promote healthy lifestyle habits.

His work focused on developing the concept, implementing the AI emotion detection model, assembling the robotic system, and testing the project to ensure it effectively encourages mental well-being and physical fitness.
