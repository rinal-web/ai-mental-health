About the Project

AI-powered innovation for mental health & fitness

Our Mission

To create an AI-powered system that promotes mental health awareness and encourages physical fitness by providing real-time emotional analysis and lifestyle recommendations.

Our Vision

To develop smart AI-based health monitoring systems that can be used in schools, homes, and healthcare environments to support wellness for people of all ages.

Core Concept

The AI Mental Health and Fitness Check Robo project uses Artificial Intelligence and robotics to monitor and support a person’s emotional and physical well-being. It detects emotions such as happy, sad, angry, or neutral, and provides supportive responses like motivational messages, relaxation suggestions, or fitness activities. At the same time, it encourages healthy habits and physical activity, guiding individuals toward a balanced lifestyle.

Why We Chose This Project

In today’s fast-paced world, many people experience stress, anxiety, and emotional pressure due to studies, work, and personal challenges. Mental health is often ignored until it becomes serious. This project aims to bring awareness and early detection of emotional changes.

  • Increasing Mental Health Issues
  • Lack of Early Detection
  • Importance of Physical Fitness
  • AI for Social Good
  • Promoting Awareness Among Students
  • Innovation in Healthcare Support
  • Contribution to Global Health Goals
