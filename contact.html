Frequently Asked Questions

What is the purpose of this project?

To promote mental health awareness and physical fitness using AI-powered emotional analysis and wellness routines.

How does the Robo detect emotions?

It uses facial recognition, voice tone analysis, and text sentiment to identify emotional states.

Can I use this in schools or homes?

Yes, the Robo is designed for use in educational, domestic, and healthcare environments.

Is my data safe and private?

Yes, all user data is handled securely and used only for wellness.

Can the Robo integrate with wearables?

Yes, it can connect with fitness trackers to provide holistic health insights.

Is there a subscription plan?

Currently, it is free for schools and healthcare pilots, with premium features planned.

Can doctors access the data?

Yes, with user consent, doctors can securely access wellness logs for better treatment.

Does it provide emergency alerts?

Yes, the Robo can send alerts to caregivers or family members in case of detected distress.

Is it suitable for children?

Yes, it includes child-friendly modes with simplified interactions and parental controls.
